That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime: The Movie - Scarlet Bond

"That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime: The Movie - Scarlet Bond" centers around a mysterious power possessed by the Queen of Raja, a small nation west of the Jura Tempest Federation.

The plot follows Rimuru Tempest, the protagonist who was reincarnated as a slime and evolved into a Demon Lord. He encounters Hiiro, a surviving member of the Ogre race, and together they unravel a long-standing conspiracy. The movie introduces new characters and emphasizes the power of bonds and friendships. The central conflict involves a corrupt Minister trying to exploit the Queen's power for his gain.

Rimuru Tempest

The background of Rimuru Tempest starts in Japan, when he was Satoru Mikami, a human.

He is stabbed to death by a criminal one day while meeting with a coworker from work.

Nevertheless, he is reborn as a slime monster with special powers in a fantasy realm called the Central World, as opposed to the majority of Otherworlders who were transferred there.

Being viewed as both male and female, he is genderless.

Shion

Shion was originally a survivor of the Ogre tribe, accompanying a small party of six that included the late chief's son and daughter through the forest.

While searching for refuge, she came across a masked majin, Rimuru, who she assumed was associated with the perpetrators that engineered the genocide of her people, and immediately attacked him.

Contrary to her expectations, she was quickly overpowered and left in awe by his power.

The ogre princess, sensing their mistake, mediated between the two parties and brought an end to their conflict.

After many twists and turns, Shion was eventually taken in by Rimuru along with her brethren and received a name, becoming his first secretary.
